Training Room Locations

Machines Training Area

Located on the 2nd floor of the Gymnasium. Equipped with:

  • Weight machines
  • Treadmills
  • Stationary bikes
  • Stretching area

Free Weights Zone

Advanced training area featuring:

  • Dumbbells (2kg - 50kg)
  • Barbells and weight plates
  • Power racks
  • Kettlebells

Usage Rules

  1. Always wear indoor sports shoes
  2. Re-rack weights after use
  3. Wipe down equipment after use
  4. Available from 7:00 am to 7:00 pm on weekdays
  5. No food or open containers
  6. Learn how to use unfamiliar equipment before use

*Please be mindful of others and maintain proper gym etiquette
